Welcome to the World of Slavic Mythology!

Immerse yourself in an exciting adventure with Lake of Ancestors, a unique strategy game that transports you to the ancient Slavic world of myths. Build your settlement, defend it from ruthless barbarians, and call upon the gods to help you defeat your enemies.

Use the Blessings of the Gods

Choose a god from the Slavic pantheon and wield their power against your foes. Crush your enemies or help your settlers develop faster.

Game Mechanics

  • Building settlements and structures;
  • Managing your army;
  • Regular attacks from AI enemies;
  • Efficient management of settlers to accelerate the development of your settlement;
  • Multiple deities with unique bonuses.
